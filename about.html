Our Goal

The goal with Motivated4Nft is to create a community of motivated individuals who share the enjoyment of all things motivation. Please note that you are buying into a community and shouldn’t expect any utility. We plan on having motivational speakers, giveaways, holder only mint pass, and much more… however, this should be seen as an additive part of the community and not the founding principles.
